Our Story

The journey that shaped who we are today

Where Passion Meets Perfection

Founded in 2010, Salon de Belle began as a small dream with a big vision. Our founder, Isabella Belle, always believed that beauty treatments should be more than just services—they should be transformative experiences that celebrate individuality.

After years of working in high-end salons across Europe, Isabella returned home with a mission: to create a sanctuary where clients could receive world-class treatments in an atmosphere of warmth and genuine care.

What started as a modest studio with just two chairs has blossomed into the premier beauty destination you see today—yet our core values remain unchanged.

"Beauty isn't about looking like someone else—it's about discovering and enhancing your own unique radiance." — Isabella Belle, Founder

Our Philosophy

At Salon de Belle, we believe that true beauty comes from a harmonious relationship between skilled technique and personalized attention. Every client who walks through our doors receives a custom experience tailored to their specific needs and desires.

We take pride in our team of dedicated professionals who continually refine their craft through ongoing education and training. Our stylists and technicians aren't just experts in their field—they're passionate artists who find joy in helping you look and feel your absolute best.

Sustainability is also at the heart of what we do. We carefully select products that are kind to both you and the environment, ensuring that beauty never comes at the expense of our planet.
